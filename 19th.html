The Electric Age Begins

Step into the late 1800s, a time of groundbreaking innovation and intense rivalry. The world stands on the brink of a technological revolution, driven by two brilliant minds: Nikola Tesla and Thomas Edison. Their fierce competition, known as the War of Currents, will determine the future of electricity.
Your Mission: As you enter the War of Currents lab, you find yourself in a bustling 19th-century workshop filled with the hum of machinery and the glow of electric lamps. The air is thick with the excitement of discovery and the tension of competition.
You must first choose your side: Will you align with Nikola Tesla, the visionary who champions Alternating Current (AC), or will you support Thomas Edison, the steadfast advocate of Direct Current (DC)? Your decision will shape your journey through this electrifying adventure.
With your allegiance decided you are tasked with a critical mission: Light Up the Bulb. Using your chosen current, you must design a circuit that successfully illuminates a bulb.
